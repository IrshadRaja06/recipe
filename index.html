Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight.

Ingredient


  • 1/2d cup Sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized aplles,peeled and sliced

Instruction


  • Preheat oven to 375*F (190*C)
  • In a bowl ,mix the sugar and cinnamon
  • Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnnamon mixture
